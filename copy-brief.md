**Section headline:** From zero to sent in five minutes.

**Step 1 — Label:** Set up your profile
**Step 1 — Body:** Enter your business name and upload your logo. Set your default currency, tax rate, and payment terms. Done once, applied everywhere.

**Step 2 — Label:** Build your invoice
**Step 2 — Body:** Select a client or create one on the spot. Add line items — or pull from your saved catalog of services and prices. Totals calculate in real time. No formulas required.

**Step 3 — Label:** Send and track
**Step 3 — Body:** Email the invoice directly from Invoicer. As payments come in, record them against the invoice. Overdue invoices surface automatically. Nothing slips through.

## Notes for Designers

- The hero eyebrow tag ("Free to start") should sit above the headline and read as a confidence signal, not a disclaimer.
- "No credit card required" should appear below CTAs wherever it fits — it removes friction at the decision point.
- The workspace section is the place to show a product screenshot. Let the UI speak.
- Status badges (draft / sent / overdue / paid) are strong visual elements — use them in feature screenshots to demonstrate clarity.
- The pricing section has only one tier for launch. Keep it simple and honest.
